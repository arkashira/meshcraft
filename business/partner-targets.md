# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### 1. **Kubernetes (K8s)**
- **Rationale**: Kubernetes is the de facto standard for container orchestration. Integrating with K8s will allow Meshcraft to manage service meshes directly within K8s clusters.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: No explicit free tier, but community edition is open-source.
- **Integration Effort**: M
- **Value-Add**: Simplifies service mesh deployment and management for K8s users.

### 2. **Istio**
- **Rationale**: Istio is a popular service mesh that provides traffic management, security, and observability. Integrating with Istio will enhance Meshcraft's capabilities.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source with no explicit free tier.
- **Integration Effort**: S
- **Value-Add**: Provides advanced traffic management and security features.

### 3. **Prometheus**
- **Rationale**: Prometheus is a widely used monitoring and alerting toolkit. Integrating with Prometheus will enable Meshcraft to provide comprehensive monitoring for service meshes.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source with no explicit free tier.
- **Integration Effort**: S
- **Value-Add**: Enhances observability and monitoring capabilities.

### 4. **Datadog**
- **Rationale**: Datadog is a monitoring and analytics platform for cloud applications. Integrating with Datadog will provide advanced monitoring and analytics for Meshcraft users.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier available with limited features.
- **Integration Effort**: M
- **Value-Add**: Provides advanced monitoring, analytics, and alerting capabilities.

### 5. **AWS EKS**
- **Rationale**: Amazon Elastic Kubernetes Service (EKS) is a managed Kubernetes service. Integrating with EKS will allow Meshcraft to manage service meshes on AWS.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier available with limited features.
- **Integration Effort**: L
- **Value-Add**: Simplifies service mesh management on AWS.

### 6. **Grafana**
- **Rationale**: Grafana is an open-source platform for monitoring and observability. Integrating with Grafana will enable Meshcraft to provide visualization and analytics for service meshes.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source with no explicit free tier.
- **Integration Effort**: S
- **Value-Add**: Provides visualization and analytics capabilities.

### 7. **HashiCorp Consul**
- **Rationale**: Consul is a service mesh solution that provides service discovery, configuration, and segmentation. Integrating with Consul will enhance Meshcraft's service mesh capabilities.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier available with limited features.
- **Integration Effort**: M
- **Value-Add**: Provides service discovery, configuration, and segmentation.

### 8. **Linkerd**
- **Rationale**: Linkerd is a lightweight service mesh for Kubernetes. Integrating with Linkerd will provide additional service mesh capabilities to Meshcraft users.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Open-source with no explicit free tier.
- **Integration Effort**: S
- **Value-Add**: Provides lightweight service mesh capabilities for Kubernetes.
