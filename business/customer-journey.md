```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** Developer or organization faces challenges in managing microservices, such as latency, security, or observability issues.
**Friction Points:**
- Lack of awareness about service mesh solutions.
- Overwhelming number of options in the service mesh landscape.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, confusion, and uncertainty.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Educational content (blogs, webinars) explaining the benefits of service mesh.
- Case studies showcasing successful implementations.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** Developer or organization starts researching service mesh solutions.
**Friction Points:**
- Difficulty in comparing different service mesh solutions.
- Concerns about complexity and learning curve.
**User Emotions:** Hopeful but cautious.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Detailed comparison guides.
- Free trials or demo environments.
**Metric:** Number of downloads or sign-ups for free trials.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Developer or organization decides to test a service mesh solution.
**Friction Points:**
- Complex setup and configuration.
- Lack of documentation or support.
**User Emotions:** Excited but anxious.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Simplified onboarding process.
- Comprehensive documentation and tutorials.
- Responsive customer support.
**Metric:** Time to first successful deployment.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Developer or organization decides to fully implement the service mesh solution.
**Friction Points:**
- Integration with existing infrastructure.
- Training and onboarding of team members.
**User Emotions:** Confident but cautious.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Seamless integration with popular tools and platforms.
- Training programs and certifications.
**Metric:** Number of production deployments.

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** Developer or organization looks to scale the service mesh solution.
**Friction Points:**
- Scalability and performance issues.
- Managing multiple environments.
**User Emotions:** Optimistic but concerned.
**Opportunities to Delight:**
- Scalability features and performance optimizations.
- Multi-environment management tools.
**Metric:** Number of additional services or environments managed.
```
