# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Meshcraft

## Problem Statement

Meshcraft is designed to address the growing complexity of service mesh management in modern cloud-native applications. As the number of microservices increases, managing service mesh configurations, traffic routing, and security becomes a significant challenge for developers and organizations. Current solutions often require manual configuration, are difficult to scale, and lack visibility into service mesh performance.

## Target Users

* Developers responsible for building and deploying cloud-native applications
* DevOps engineers tasked with managing service mesh configurations and performance
* Organization leaders seeking to improve the efficiency and scalability of their service mesh management

## Goals

* Provide a scalable and efficient service mesh management solution that simplifies configuration and deployment
* Offer real-time visibility into service mesh performance and traffic routing
* Enable developers and DevOps engineers to quickly identify and resolve service mesh issues
* Improve the overall efficiency and scalability of service mesh management

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### High Priority (Must-Haves)

1. **Automated Service Mesh Configuration**: Meshcraft will automatically generate and manage service mesh configurations based on application requirements.
2. **Real-time Traffic Routing**: Meshcraft will provide real-time visibility into traffic routing and enable developers to easily manage traffic flow.
3. **Service Mesh Performance Monitoring**: Meshcraft will offer real-time monitoring and alerting for service mesh performance issues.

### Medium Priority (Should-Haves)

1. **Service Mesh Security**: Meshcraft will provide features for secure service mesh communication, including encryption and authentication.
2. **Integration with Popular Cloud Providers**: Meshcraft will support integration with popular cloud providers, including AWS, GCP, and Azure.
3. **Multi-Cluster Support**: Meshcraft will enable management of multiple service mesh clusters from a single interface.

### Low Priority (Nice-to-Haves)

1. **AI-Powered Service Mesh Optimization**: Meshcraft will offer AI-powered optimization for service mesh performance and resource utilization.
2. **Customizable Dashboards**: Meshcraft will provide customizable dashboards for service mesh performance and traffic routing.
3. **Integration with Popular CI/CD Tools**: Meshcraft will support integration with popular CI/CD tools, including Jenkins and GitLab CI/CD.

## Success Metrics

* Adoption rate: 20% of top 100 cloud-native companies adopt Meshcraft within the first 6 months
* Customer satisfaction: 90% of customers report improved efficiency and scalability in service mesh management
* Revenue growth: $1M in revenue within the first year, with a growth rate of 20% per quarter

## Scope

Meshcraft will be a cloud-native application built using a microservices architecture. It will be designed to support multiple service mesh protocols, including Istio and Linkerd. Meshcraft will be developed using a modular design, with each feature built as a separate module.

## Out-of-Scope

* Development of custom service mesh protocols
* Integration with legacy systems or non-cloud-native applications
* Development of custom CI/CD pipelines or deployment scripts

## Assumptions and Dependencies

* Meshcraft will be built using a cloud-native architecture and will be designed to support multiple service mesh protocols.
* Meshcraft will be developed using a modular design, with each feature built as a separate module.
* Meshcraft will be deployed on a cloud provider of choice (AWS, GCP, or Azure).

## Risks and Mitigation Strategies

* Risk: Complexity of service mesh management may be too high for some customers.
* Mitigation: Provide extensive documentation and support for customers, and offer training and certification programs for developers and DevOps engineers.
* Risk: Competition from established service mesh management solutions may be too strong.
* Mitigation: Focus on providing a unique value proposition, such as AI-powered optimization and customizable dashboards, and offer competitive pricing and support.
